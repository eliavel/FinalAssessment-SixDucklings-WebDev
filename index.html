Street Fighter 6 Roster Profiles

The Ultimate Fighting Arena

Street Fighter 6 represents the latest evolution of Capcom's iconic fighting franchise. Powered by the RE Engine, the game fuses classic 2D tactical combat with a high-energy, vibrant urban art style heavily influenced by street culture and hip-hop graffiti.

Whether exploring the sprawling single-player metro mode, hanging out in the virtual arcade lobbies, or competing on the global ranked ladder, the game offers a feature-rich experience engineered for both fighting game veterans and casual newcomers alike.

The Drive Gauge System A multi-segmented meter managing tactical mechanics like Drive Impact, Drive Rush, Parries, and Overdrive arts.
Accessible Controls Features classic 6-button layouts alongside streamlined Modern and Dynamic control configurations for easier execution.

Luke

The Challenger of Tomorrow

Description: A contractor for a private military company, Luke uses his elite MMA background to teach modern martial arts. He loves PC gaming, junk food, and a good scrap.

Strengths: Phenomenal projectiles (Sand Blast), incredible corner carry, and highly optimal, consistent target combos.

Weaknesses: Perfect-timing requirements on his Flash Knuckle strings are mandatory for maximizing his damage output.

Jamie

The Chinatown Peacemaker

Description: A breakdancing martial artist inspired by the legendary Twin Dragons. He defends his town with a specialized herbal drink that unlocks his true kung fu potential.

Strengths: Gets progressively stronger throughout the round. Gated movesets unlock new target combos, command grabs, and divekicks.

Weaknesses: Starts at "Drink Level 0" with scaling penalties to his damage output, making his early-round pressure weak.

Chun-Li

The Blue Jade

Description: A former Interpol investigator who now runs kung fu classes. She remains one of the strongest, most respected martial artists on the planet.

Strengths: Mastery of neutral space, incredible mid-range pokes, and complex mixups utilizing her unique Serenity Stance.

Weaknesses: High execution barrier due to charge motions and stance-switching combos required to optimize her neutral wins.

The Purple Thrill-Seeker

Description: A sadistic Taekwondo practitioner who thrives on battle adrenaline. With the Feng Shui Engine gone, she fights purely for chaotic fun and personal gratification.

Ryu

The Eternal Wanderer

Description: Having conquered the Satsui no Hado, Ryu continues to travel the globe dressed in a traditional hermit's kasaya, seeking the true meaning of strength.

Strengths: Massive single-hit damage, a versatile fireball game, and lethal punish-counter combos with Hashogeki.

Dead or Alive 6

Dead or Alive 6 (DOA6) is a 3D fighting game developed by Team Ninja and Koei Tecmo. Known for its accessible "triangle system" (strikes beat throws, throws beat holds, holds beat strikes), it features fast-paced combat, stage hazards, and a revamped updated version called DOA6 Last Round.

Kasumi

The Kunoichi of Destiny

Description: Runaway shinobi and master of Mugen Tenshin ninjutsu. She fights to protect her clan while running from her past.

Insane frame speed, deadly mid-low mixups, and highly evasive teleports.

Weakness: Low base strike damage; heavily reliant on basic strings which can become predictable.

Ryu Hayabusa

The Ultimate Ninja

Description: A legendary ninja warrior who commands the Hayabusa clan. He works from the shadows to maintain world balance.

Devastating Izuna Drop command grabs, excellent aerial mobility, and massive comeback potential.

Weakness: High execution barrier; many heavy moves are risky and unsafe on block.

Helena Douglas

The Fortune Goddess

Description: The elegant president of DOATEC, who uses her Pi Qua Quan martial arts prowess to manage tournament corruption.

Low-profile Bokuho stance safely slips under high moves. Fluid, relentless pressure strings.

Weakness: Weak tracking options against opponents who heavily utilize side-stepping.

Momiji

The Shrine Maiden

Description: Ryu Hayabusa's apprentice and the Dragon Shrine Maiden tasked with protecting ancient, sacred relics.

Exceptional air juggling, unique double jump setups, and long-range spacing pokes.

Weakness: Lacks fast close-range "panic button" strikes when cornered under pressure.
